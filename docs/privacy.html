Privacy Policy

App: Journal  ·  Effective date: May 24, 2026  ·  Last updated: May 24, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the Journal mobile application (the “App”) handles your information. The App is a personal journaling tool that runs entirely on your device.

1. Information we collect

None. The App does not collect, transmit, or store any personal information on any server we control or have access to. We do not have user accounts, we do not require an email address, and we do not ask for any permissions beyond biometric authentication for unlocking the App on your device.

2. Where your journal entries are stored

All journal entries you write are saved in an encrypted database on your device only. The encryption key is generated on first launch and stored in your device’s secure hardware-backed keystore (Android Keystore on Android, Keychain on iOS). Neither the encryption key nor your entries leave your device.

3. Network access

Release builds of the App distributed through the Google Play Store and Apple App Store do not request the INTERNET permission on Android. The App is technically incapable of contacting any server, sending analytics, or transmitting your journal content over a network.

4. Third-party services

The App does not include any third-party analytics, advertising, crash reporting, or tracking software development kits (SDKs). No information about you or your usage is shared with any third party.

5. Biometric authentication

The App uses your device’s built-in biometric authentication (fingerprint, Face ID, or device PIN/passcode) to unlock the App. Biometric data is processed entirely by your device’s operating system. The App never sees, stores, or transmits your biometric data.

6. Backup and export

The App provides a Backup feature that creates a ZIP archive of your journal entries as plain Markdown files and opens your device’s share menu so you can save the backup wherever you choose. What happens to that file once it leaves the App depends on the service or app you send it to — it is outside the control of this App, and its handling is governed by that service’s privacy policy.

7. Children’s privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13 (or the applicable minimum age in your jurisdiction). Because the App collects no information from anyone, this is true by design.

8. Data deletion

All of your journal data is stored on your device. To delete it, uninstall the App from your device. There is no cloud copy and no account to close.

9. Changes to this policy

If we make material changes to this Privacy Policy, we will update this page and revise the “Last updated” date at the top. Continued use of the App after such an update constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

10. Contact
