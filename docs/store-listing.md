## Long description

```
The world's simplest journal. The world's fastest way to capture a thought.

Open the app. Touch the fingerprint. You're already writing — the cursor is
blinking on today's page before you've even decided what to say. Two taps from
locked to written.

Most journaling apps pile on streaks, prompts, reminders, and moods until
journaling itself feels like a chore. Journal does the opposite. One screen,
one job: making it effortless to capture a moment. No accounts to create, no
settings to wade through. Just you, a blinking cursor, and what's on your mind.
Write now, find later.

HOW IT WORKS
Open the app → touch the fingerprint sensor → write. That's the whole flow.
Every keystroke saves itself, so there's never a button to press or a thought
to lose.

FEATURES
• Two-tap opening — tap the icon, touch your fingerprint, start writing
• Fingerprint unlock, with automatic fall-back to your device PIN/passcode
• Auto-save as you type — you can never lose a word
• Full-text search across every entry you've ever written
• Automatic session timestamps each time you sit down to write
• Past entries, organized by date
• Auto-locks after 5 minutes in the background — your journal isn't sitting
  open if your phone is

PRIVACY
• Every entry is stored in an encrypted database on your device
• The encryption key never leaves your phone — it lives in your device's
  hardware-backed keystore
• The app has no internet permission. Your journal physically cannot reach a
  server — ours or anyone else's
• Every entry stays on this phone — no cloud, by design
• Back up anytime: export your whole journal as plain, readable Markdown files
  you control. Lose or change phones, and your words come with you. No lock-in.
• No account, no sign-up, no email
• No analytics, no trackers, no ads

This is a journal that respects your privacy and your time.
Nothing more, nothing less.

Open. Touch. Write.
```
